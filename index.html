    Project idea

    The project idea is a new mail system that can be used when people purchase item online. When purchases are made online a tracking number is given however this only shows the predicted day the order will be delivered, my idea is for each delivery to have a mini GPS tracker that will give users the exact location where their delivery is and how far away it is from their home including how many days or hours it will be. This new system will also include the option of choosing three days when they want it delivered and to digitally write their signature using a code sent to their phone, this mail system will be in form of a new app.

    Being an online shopper myself, I dislike not knowing the exact day and time my delivery will come especially if I’m not home and since most websites need a signature it means having to go to the post office. According to statista 1.79 billion people purchased something online, with the top three being clothes, shoes and electronic appliances most needing a signature, a lot of people also choose to buy furniture or appliances that will have someone coming to install it this why by having a system that not only gives them the day but also time it saves people having to wait around for their delivery to come and for the people that work all day by giving them the option to choose the day or to give their sign signature online allowing them to not have to take the trip to the post office especially for the people in areas which may not have many close by.

    The mail system will have two aspects an app in which the user will use which will give them all their information and allow them to complete aspects of it and a miniature GPS system which will be placed in their parcel giving them the exact location and time of where their parcel is. When a person purchases their item, they can choose to use this system when choosing delivery option at a extra cost, they would need to make a account ensuring they have the correct address and tracking number. This system can be used for all items across the world however not all aspects will not be available. The main feature is the tracking system which will gives them a map of where their delivery is at any time of the day and will be updated at all times it will give them the exact day the delivery comes and a time (For example will arrive in 36 hours, 7 minutes approximately 12:36 on Tuesday 3rd) however if delays do happen they will be notified immediately, by giving them the time and day it allows them to organise someone to be home if it is a big delivery or one that needs a signature, also for the people that are waiting impatiently it will let them know it is coming soon.

    This does not take the risk of delays however they will be notified when normally they won’t. Another aspect is choosing a day in which this will be delivered this will only be available for items needing a signature or which will be needing to be installed however to take the risk of delays or circumstances making it late, the user will be give a option of three days and in most cases will be their first option however due to the availability of mail workers they can not choose the exact time or weekends/public holidays. This will be done through the app and will only become available when their delivery is at the closest post office or delivery point there is a option to cancel of change days but this must be done by 8am the next morning. For smaller items (Does not include electrical appliances or purchases made over $300 for security reasons) they will have the opportunity to write their signature only if they are not home this will be done through the app and they will be notified of this on delivery day however to ensure this is secure they will be given a code sent to their phone that they must put in they must also answer three security questions they put in when registering to use this, however to make sure security is used the code will change each day this allow for their delivery to be left somewhere safe however for large purchases they will need someone to be home due to the safety of leaving something valuable out the front door this is why they are given the day and time so they can have someone waiting to collect it for them however the person who purchased will need to do the signature on line and leave the details of the person collection unless they are from the same address or they are given the choice of days so they can ensure they are home.

    The tracker for the parcels will be a pre made Mini GPS tracker or Mini GPS GSM tracker that will be put in the parcel and can be replaceable. The software needed to make the app will be an app developer such as appery or MIT app developer that will make the app available on android, apple and windows, programming will also be needed. The skills required would mainly be app development and programming the app to be capable of completing the tasks that are needed, as the app will involve a lot including a tracking system that will need to be connected to a tracker, skills will be needed to ensure that is possible and that the app will run properly. The app will also need to be connected the postage system, so there will be a level of challenge to develop this.

    The successful outcome would be that the tracking of the delivery will be accurate and that the time and date will be correct, the issues to begin with can be issues with the tracking system but overtime this should not be an issue and users will be content about the time and day of their delivery. The other aspects such as choosing the date will be successful if cancellations and change of days only happen due to extreme weather or circumstances out of the post offices control. Another issue could be the security of the online signature which some people may be hesitant to use however by ensuring security answers must be answer and a code is sent this can make people less cautious to use it.
